This project is to make a cloth simulator with collison, wind, raining and flaming effect.


Programming Language & API: Javascript, OpenGL
My part: proj3_sim.js, proj3_webgl.js, proj3.html


Ball xyz sliders adjust velocity
Ball position is reset with full reset
Ball radius adjusts the size of the ball
Burn start is updated on reset
Rain and burn are triggered with their tick marks

Project link: https://gilberttang.github.io/Cloth_Simulation/

Check your browser support WebGL or not: 
http://webglreport.com/?v=1
